Current issues in the pharmaceutical industry
Protein prediction is crucial in modern medicine, particularly in precision medicine and drug development. Proteins are key molecules that perform various functions in organisms, and protein prediction uses computer modeling to predict their 3D structure and interactions, helping to understand their roles in cells and the body. This process offers valuable insights into the causes of diseases.
